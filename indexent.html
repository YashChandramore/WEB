AAMIR KHAN LAUNCHED KAHANI SONG

The much awaited film of the year 2022 LAAL SINGH CHADDA and Aamir khan launched title song of this film through radio station. This song is released in audio form . From past few days , Aamir khan was uploading videos on social media and said that he would told a kahani on 28 april and he fulfilled his promise.
